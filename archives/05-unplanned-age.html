Unplanned Age

I did not plan to live this long.

That sentence is not here for drama. It is here because it is structurally true.

Some version of me did not imagine this age. Did not prepare a map for it. Did not write instructions. Did not leave a clean folder structure.

So now I am here, older than expected, and sometimes I do not know what to do with the extra years.

That can feel absurd. Like waking up inside a building after the blueprint ended.

I am here, but not always oriented. I am functional, but not always convinced. I am building, but I do not always know toward what.

The strange thing is that I still care.

I care about people. I care about not becoming cruel. I care about making things less painful. I care about tools being understandable. I care about small repairs.

Maybe that is enough for now.

Boris
