DOCUMENT 03 / STATUS: UNSTABLE

Neuro-Weather

Some minds do not move in straight lines.

Mine often behaves like weather.

Attention arrives, disappears, breaks open, floods the wrong room, refuses the important door, becomes obsessed with a detail that nobody else saw, then abandons the entire building without notice.

ADHD is not only distraction. Sometimes it is too much signal. Sometimes it is a control panel where every light is blinking and none of the labels are reliable.

The autistic shape of my mind is different. It can be precise. It can be intense. It can notice patterns, structures, inconsistencies, small violences hidden in ordinary systems.

But it can also flood.

Too much noise. Too much ambiguity. Too many invisible rules. Too many people pretending the interface is obvious.

And under that, there is the old low current of dysthymia.

Not always a storm. More like a machine left running in another room. You hear it less on good days. It does not mean it stopped.

LLow-current machine remains audible.
