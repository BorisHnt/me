The Void Is Not Empty

The void is not always an enemy.

Sometimes it is only the space where no instruction was written.

Sometimes it is the room behind the room. Sometimes it is the silence after the machine stops asking for input. Sometimes it is what remains when every obvious version of the future has failed to load.

I used to think emptiness meant absence. Now I am less sure.

Some empty spaces are hostile. Some are unfinished. Some are waiting for a shape.

I am still here.

The document cannot explain why.

Maybe the sentence was never a cure. Maybe it was only a maintenance command.
