DOCUMENT 04 / STATUS: EMOTIONALLY UNSAFE

Cathedral of Wanting

Limerence is difficult to describe without making it sound more beautiful than it is.

It is not simply love. It is not devotion. It is not proof of depth.

It is the mind building a cathedral around a person who never asked to become architecture.

A look becomes evidence. A silence becomes a room. A message becomes weather. An absence becomes a machine.

The embarrassing part is not only the intensity. It is the way the mind starts producing meaning faster than reality can correct it.

I try to stay responsible inside that distortion.

The other person is not a symbol. Not a cure. Not a missing piece. Not a door.

They are a person.

That sentence has to remain stronger than the cathedral.

F / R / O / M — recovered only after the lower corridor is crossed.

Boris
